Reserve Price

Sellers can set a reserve price, which is the minimum amount they're willing to sell the item for. If the highest bid doesn't meet the reserve, the item won't be sold. The reserve price is not visible to bidders, but they'll be informed if the reserve has been met.

Automatic Bidding

Our system allows you to set a maximum bid amount. The system will then automatically bid on your behalf, increasing your bid by the minimum increment necessary to keep you as the highest bidder, up to your maximum amount.

Sniping Protection

To prevent last-second "sniping" (bidding in the final seconds), auctions will be automatically extended by 5 minutes if a bid is placed in the last minute of the auction.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I win an auction but change my mind?

+

When you place a bid, you're making a commitment to purchase the item if you win. If you don't complete the purchase, you may receive negative feedback and your account could face restrictions. In extreme cases, you may be banned from the platform.

How do I know if an item is authentic?

+

We encourage sellers to provide provenance and authentication for valuable items. For high-value categories like art, watches, and collectibles, many sellers offer certificates of authenticity. Additionally, our buyer protection covers cases of misrepresentation or counterfeit goods.

What should I do if I don't receive my item or it's not as described?

+

Contact the seller first to resolve the issue. If you can't reach a resolution, you can open a dispute through our platform within 14 days of the estimated delivery date. Our support team will review the case and may issue a refund if necessary.

Can I sell internationally?

+

Yes, you can specify in your listing whether you're willing to ship internationally. Be aware of customs regulations and potential duties that buyers may need to pay in different countries.
